
[font_size=24]INFO ABOUT PALETTES[/font_size]

Base palette is [code]res://palette.png[/code]. DO NOT EDIT IT.

Other palettes can be edited.

Materials are in [code]res://materials/[/code].

Each material points to the palette png it uses.
